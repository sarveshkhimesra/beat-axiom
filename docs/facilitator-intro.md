# THE DEAL — Rahul's opening (read this before Round 1)

Sets the tone. Keep it warm and a little theatrical. ~90 seconds.

> "Welcome to **THE DEAL**. For the next hour you are not yourselves — you are payment
> companies fighting for one deal with NovaBrand, India's hottest D2C brand. AXIOM
> scores every move. One team goes home each round. One of you wins.
>
> A few things to keep in mind — this is how you win:
>
> 1. **It's a real conversation. Talk to them.** You speak; they speak back. No typing,
>    no decks. Treat the people across the table as *partners*, not targets — there are
>    only a handful of serious payment players in the country, and they know it.
>
> 2. **Warmth wins points.** Build rapport. Find common ground. The team that makes a
>    genuine human connection scores higher — that's not a soft skill here, it's the game.
>
> 3. **Watch the screen.** Between every round, AXIOM drops a **secret** on the projector.
>    The teams that catch it and use it in the next round pull ahead. Miss it and you're
>    flying blind.
>
> 4. **One nudge:** if you just paste an AI's answer back at them, it shows — and it
>    won't win you the room. Use your own heads. *(This is on your honour — not in the
>    scoring. But trust me, canned answers lose.)*
>
> Have fun with it. Be sharp, be human. Let the game begin."

Notes:
- Point 4 is a **nudge only** — there is no AI-detection in the scoring code, by design.
- Secrets, by round: R1→R2 the live **checkout**; R2→R3 **forward-deployed engineers**;
  R3→R4 the shared **investor / board member (Mayur)**; R4→R5 **competitor intel** (may be
  true or false — judgement call).
- Facilitator console: `extend [secs]` or the **+ 2:00** button adds overtime; the round
  only ends when you hit **End Round** (responses keep counting past 0:00).
